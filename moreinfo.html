InCont Travel Agency

InCont Travel Agency, based in Oslo, Norway, revolves around the idea of selling affordable travel packages to people. We believe that a lot of people avoid taking big city vacations because they are way to expensive. So, we decided to sell two different packages where you will experience the most out of each city, way cheaper than our competitors. These are the packages our customers can choose from:

The History Package: Rome & London.
This trip will focus mostly on history and landmarks. Museum trips with the most experienced guides is to be expected.
The Experience Package: New York & Tokyo.
This trip will focus mostly on seeing the cultural life of these beautiful cities. We will show you the most famous landmarks, the best restaurants, and even talk to locals!
